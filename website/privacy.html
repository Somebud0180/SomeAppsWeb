Privacy Policy

Your data isn't important to me.
We don't store any data on any server. We have no server.

The app only collects necessary data for it to function, used to store your trackers and groups. (names, symbols, colors, etc.) All data collected by the app is stored on your device and does not leave your device.1 2

I do not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties. The app does not collect analytics. If you have any questions about my privacy policy, please feel free to contact me.

1 Except where iCloud Sync is utilized, wherein data will pass through Apple's servers to sync data across your devices.
2 Data you choose to export via the app's export function will be stored or shared to where you choose.
