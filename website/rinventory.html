rInventory

rInventory is a simple yet usefeul app to help you track of your items. Place your items in a digital inventory, keep track of their quantities, locations, and categories with customizable features. Sort the items or control the main view to make it easier to get what you need.

rThink the Inventory

A game inventory but for real life

Store, Sort, Delight

Organize your items with categorization, locations, and images. Keep track of quantities, and find what you need quickly. Perfect for home organization, inventory management, and keeping track of your stuff.

Items in your Inventory

View your items up close. Each item can have a name, quantity, location, category, and a symbol or image. Easily add, edit, or remove items from your inventory.

Search your Inventory

Filter and search through your items with ease. Use the search bar to quickly find what you need, or browse through categories and locations.

Sort your Inventory

Adjust the home view to your liking. Customize the rows visible and keep only what you need at the top. Choose how you want to see your items, whether by your sort order, alphabetical order, or modification date.

Across Devices

From your different devices, big and small. See and manage your inventory. Future-ready with Liquid Glass whilst still looking sleek in previous operating systems.
